---
title: "Members"
description: "Manage your Cogfy Messenger workspaces members"
---

Workspace members are users who have been invited to collaborate within a specific Cogfy Messenger workspace. Each member can have different roles and permissions, allowing for effective team collaboration and management of conversations, contacts, and integrations.

> To manage workspace members, visit [messenger.cogfy.com/:WORKSPACE/members](https://messenger.cogfy.com/:WORKSPACE/members).

## Deleting members

Click "<Icon icon="trash" color='red' iconType="solid" />" to remove a member from the workspace. Please note that only workspace admins have the permission to delete members.
